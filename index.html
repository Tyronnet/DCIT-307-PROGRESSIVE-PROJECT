NASOD

NATIONAL SECRETRAIT for ONLINE DRIVERS

NASOD stands for NATIONAL SECRETRAIT for ONLINE DRIVERS. This is a body which houses all Associations formed by ride hailing drivers in Ghana. It comprises drivers from all regions where all the ridehailing companies such as Uber, Bolt,Yango and others operate.
